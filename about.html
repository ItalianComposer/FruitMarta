About us

Looking for a gentle and faithful kitten-like Maine coon, Scottish or British kitten? Or do you want an independent, strong, and exotic kitten? Or maybe a playful Maltese puppy?

We at MeoWoof offer you to help find your perfect pet for your family. Your new friend will give you much love and joy.

To accomplish this mission and goal we cooperate with the best European catteries such as Albostar*UA, Bastetto*UA, Romanoff Hause. All catteries are officially registered by WCF International.

Parents of our kittens and puppies take part in exhibitions according to the WCF, TICA, CFA, and ICU systems and have high marks from experts from different countries. At a certain age, cattery producers undergo ultrasound testing for genetic diseases such as polycystic kidney disease and cardiomyopathy (PKD & HCM).

Also, every year all parents are tested in licensed laboratories for viral diseases: viral immunodeficiency of cats (FIV), viral leukemia (FeLV), coronoviral gastroenteritis (FeCoV), etc..

On request, you will be given genetic tests from the parents of your chosen pet.

All our pets grow without cages, in love, and care.

Why did we decide to combine cats and dogs? Because it’s a myth that they can’t be friends! They become best friends who live in the same house! By allowing you to choose both a kitten and a puppy at the same time, we make it easier for you to find and spend time online. Our website contains a selection of the friendliest kitten and puppy breeds.

Our main goal at MeoWoof is to find the perfect family for our pets and help you choose your best friend. Our pets are therapeutic animals. They bring a lot of happiness and love to your family.

In addition, studies show that when a child has no sibling, pets help them develop greater empathy, which can lead to increased self-esteem and possible increased participation in social and physical activities.

Let us help you with this, All MeoWoof pets are vaccinated against feline infections, such as feline panleukopenia (fever), viral rhinotracheitis, calicivirus, and rabies. All chipped.
